The Past

The NBA is rich in history and traditions, founded in 1946 as the Basketball Association of America.

With over 70 years of players and teams, the story of the NBA has plenty to offer.

Learn about the Legends:

  • Bill Russell
  • David Stern
  • Phil Jackson
  • Michael Jordan

The Present

The NBA has more talent than ever, which makes for a better viewing experience than ever.

7 different organizations have won a championship in the past 9 years, meaning the title is up for grabs every year.

The league is enticing for fans new and old, with players more talented than ever.

The Legends of Now:

  • LeBron James
  • Kevin Durant
  • Stephen Curry
  • Damian Lillard

The Future

As the talent in basketball continues to flourish, so do its young superstars.

Due to the global growth of the sport, the NBA now has more foreign-born players than it's ever had.

The NBA has a very bright future.

The Legends to Come:

  • Giannis Antetokounmpo
  • Luka Doncic
  • Joel Embiid
  • Ja Morant
